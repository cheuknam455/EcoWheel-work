How It Works

EcoWheel is an easy and eco-friendly way to rent a bike! Simply sign up through our app or website and locate your closest bike station using our map.

Pick a bike, scan the QR code on the bike with our app, and ride off. Enjoy a carefree time as you explore the city or commute to your destination.

After you're done, return the bike to the closest EcoWheel station. EcoWheel is an affordable and convenient way to travel. Join us and ride smart, save money, and reduce your carbon footprint!
